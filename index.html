Consultancy History

Meet Sumitra

Sumithra Bala, who grew up in England but now lives with her family in Sydney, founded the company in 2004, following a ten year career with UNICEF. She is a development professional with nearly 20 years of international experience in the humanitarian and development field. She holds a Master of Science degree in Social Policy (Melbourne, Australia) and an undergraduate honours degree in International Development (Sussex, UK).

About »

Programme Design

Since 2004, BALA Consulting has provided significant strategic planning support to UNICEF Country Offices (COs) to develop and adjust Country Programmes - drafting Country Programme Documents (CPDs), Country Programme Action Plans (CPAPs), Mid Term Reviews (MTRs), Rolling Work Plans (RWPS), Resource Mobilisation Strategies and Annual Reports. The purpose of all these forums has been to assist UNICEF COs through identifying best practices to develop and refine equity strategies and also to promote a greater understanding and ownership of the equity agenda with Governments and civil society.
